Frequently Asked Questions

How does Wingie offer the cheapest flight tickets?

Wingie compares hundreds of flights from airlines based on your destination and sorts them by cheapest. The site displays a list of the most suitable flights for your destination on your planned travel date, as well as the latest daily estimated prices using a price chart. This way you can see the cheapest flight date, and if your travel date is flexible you can get a ticket at a lower price.

How does Wingie ensure the security of my transactions?

Wingie protects all your transactions on the site with an SSL certificate. All the information you enter when booking airline tickets and the bank or credit card information you save in your account are protected by PCI DSS compliant organizations. In addition, there is a Secure3D system when making the payment process. So you can book all your tickets safely on Wingie.

Can I find all the airlines and flights I'm looking for on Wingie?

When searching for airline tickets on Wingie, you can find direct or stopover flights to your destination scheduled by the airlines. With our LogiConnect feature, you can also see flights we have created that are cheaper or have a shorter flight time than other sites.

How can I book a flight ticket on Wingie?

You can use our website to book flight tickets from Wingie. Simply enter your destination and flight date and the website will display hundreds of flights for airlines, you can compare them and choose the most suitable flight for you and book your ticket safely and quickly.
